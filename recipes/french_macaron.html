French Macaron Recipe

The World-Famous French Macaron

The French macaron that you're about to create is an extremely delicate and colorful (pink, in this case) confection that doesn't require extraordinary culinary skills for you to bake.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of almond flour
  • 3 white eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3 drops of food coloring
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 1 1/2 cups of confectioners' sugar
  • 1/4 cup of superfine sugar

Steps for the creation of your delicious French Macaron

  1. Preheat the oven to 320 degrees. Whisk confectioners' and almond flour.
  2. Sift the sugar-almond flour mixture, little by little, through a fine-mesh sieve into a bowl, pressing with a rubber spatula to pass through as much as possible
  3. Beat the eggs whites, cream of tartar and your pinch of salt until frothy. (You can use a mixer or a whisker). Gradually add superfine sugar and beat until stiff and shiny.
  4. Pour the egg whites into the bowl with almond flour.
  5. Add food coloring, please add pink because you CLEARLY came here for pink macarons, right?
