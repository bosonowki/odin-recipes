Vova's Egg Fried Rice

Vova's Delicious Egg Fried Rice

This, ladies and gentlemen, is definitely not Askar's Shakshuka, but it can compete with it on a scale of deliciousness.
This recipe is meant for students who are a bit too lazy to cook proper meals.
So, if you're a student who has no idea how to cook, this recipe is perfect for you.

Ingredients

  • 200g of boiled rice (preferrably cold)
  • 4-5 eggs
  • Soy sauce
  • Sunflower oil

Steps for the creation of Vova's Egg Fried Rice

  1. Put your pan on medium heat and pour some sunflower oil. Spread it across the whole pan.
    This is done in order for your cooking not to stick to the pan, so cleaning it will be much easier.
  2. Drop your boiled rice onto the pan, spread it out a bit with a spatula.
  3. While the rice is there, whisk your eggs in a bowl and once you're done, this should be the perfect spread it across your rice.
  4. Occasionally mix in order for the eggs and rice to fry equally.
  5. After a couple minutes of mixing, add about two tablespoons of soy sauce. However, if you add to much, the meal will turn out to be too salty.
  6. Cover the lid and wait for your masterpiece to be done.
