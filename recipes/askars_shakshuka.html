Askar's Shakshuka Recipe

Undisputed, Undefeated Askar's Shakshuka

This, ladies and gentlemen, is one of the greatest recipes you've ever seen. The amount of dopamine your brain will produce after a single bite of Askar's shakshuka will leave you happy and your stomach full for countless hours.

Ingredients (Serves 2-3)

  • 1 large onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 5-6 eggs
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • salt, black pepper, paprika
  • 3 tbs of tomato sauce (preferrable dense)
  • 4-5 fresh tomatoes

Steps for the creation of Askar's Shakshuka

  1. Prepare all your ingredients: finely dice onion and garlic; if using tomatoes for sauce, peel them and dice (it will lose liquid when dicing, make sure you're able to preserve it; dice bell peppers into small bitesize pieces.
  2. Throw in your dices onion and garlic on a medium-high heat pan.
  3. Once the onion becomes yellow-transparent (usually in 2-3 minutes) throw in your bitesized bell peppers.
  4. After 2-3 minutes, throw in tomato paste and tomatoes/sauce. Add salt, black pepper and paprika to your taste.
  5. Lower the heat to medium, cover the pan with a lid, observe and mix ocasionally. If need be, add a little bit of water to create more sauce.
  6. Once it reaches a certain consistency, remove the lid, make 5-6 holes with a spoon and crack an egg in each hole.
  7. Cover the lid, wait until done.
  8. Enjoy the undisputed, undefeated Askar's shakshuka!
